Our Craft

From Green Bean to Cup

Every step in our process honors the work that came before. From the farmers who nurture each plant to the roasters who coax out its potential.

Sourcing

We travel to origin, cupping hundreds of samples to find lots that express their terroir with clarity and depth. Direct relationships mean fair prices for farmers.

Direct Trade Sample Roasting

Resting

Green coffee needs time to stabilize after its journey. We rest each lot in climate-controlled storage, allowing the moisture to equalize.

Climate Control 30+ Days Rest

Roasting

Small batches in our vintage Probat allow precise control. We develop profiles that highlight each origin's unique character—never under, never over.

Small Batch Profile Roasting

Delivery

Roasted to order and shipped within 24 hours. Each bag is hand-labeled with the roast date, origin, and tasting notes—because you deserve to know.

Roast to Order Hand Labeled
